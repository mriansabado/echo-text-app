## Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

### Is my data collected?
No, absolutely not! PocketSay is completely offline and private. We collect zero data, track nothing, and your messages never leave your device.

### Do I need an account?
No account required! Just download and start using PocketSay immediately.

### Does this work on iPad?
Yes! PocketSay works great on both iPhone and iPad, with full support for all screen sizes.

### What's the character limit?
You can enter up to 68 characters per message for optimal readability at a distance.

### Are there subscriptions or ads?
No! PocketSay is a one-time purchase of $0.99 with no subscriptions, no ads, and no recurring fees.

### Can I customize the display?
Absolutely! You can customize fonts, backgrounds (including custom photos), text colors, animations, and day/night modes.

### Can I use my own photos as backgrounds?
Yes! PocketSay includes a custom photo background feature. Simply select a photo from your library to use as your background.

### Text not displaying?
Make sure you've entered text and tapped the submit button. Try rotating to landscape mode for the best display.

### Background not showing?
Check that you've selected a background theme or custom photo. Try switching between day and night modes.

### Landscape not working?
Ensure your device rotation lock is off. PocketSay automatically switches to landscape when displaying messages.

### Colors not changing?
Make sure you've selected different colors for day and night modes. The colors are separate for each mode.

### Is my data private?
Yes! PocketSay collects zero data, requires no internet connection, and all your messages stay on your device. Complete privacy guaranteed.
